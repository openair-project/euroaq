# euroaq: Access Air Quality Data from the EEA Air Quality Download Service

Access data from the European Environment Agency's Air Quality Download
Service (<https://eeadmz1-downloads-webapp.azurewebsites.net/>) by
providing convenient R wrappers for all of their API endpoints.
